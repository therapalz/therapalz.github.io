Therapalz

A therapeutic plush designed to help caretakers better care for individuals with Alzheimer's.

About The Project

Purpose

Therapalz will provide tools for caregivers to de-stress Alzheimer's patients in everyday situations. Our goal is to build a product that can comfort individuals with Alzheimer's in moments of anxiety or high stress.

Features

Right now Therapalz is in the form of a stuffed animal. We've packed some electronics inside which allows a Therapal to purr like a cat when it's petted! It also gives off a gentle amount of heat for optimized snuggling.

Future Goals

We are hard at work polishing our prototype to ensure it functions reliably. Our next steps include deeper interaction studies with users in Spring 2016. Stay tuned!

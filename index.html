Therapalz

A therapeutic plush designed to help caretakers better care for individuals with Alzheimer's.

About The Project

Purpose

Therapalz is a platform that will be provide multiple tools for caregivers to destress patients with Alzheimer's in everyday situations. Our goal with Therapalz is to provide a product that may offer relief and comfort in moments of anxiety or high stress among individuals with Alzheimer's.

Features

Right now Therapalz is in the form of a stuffed animal (pictures to the right). We've packed some electronics inside which allow for a Therapal to react to petting interactions, it purrs like a real cat! There's also a gentle amount of heat Therapalz gives off for optimized snuggling.

Future Goals

We are hard at work making Therapalz function reliably. Our next steps include deeper interaction studies with users in Spring 2016. Stay tuned!

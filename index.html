Therapalz

A collective of students designing products and services
to help improve the quality of life for at-home caregivers.

Current Work

Purpose

Therapalz will provide tools for caregivers to de-stress Alzheimer's patients in everyday situations. Our goal is to build a product that can comfort individuals with Alzheimer's in moments of anxiety or high stress.

Robotic Animal-Assisted Therapy

This idea was hatched Fall of 2015, and the goal in mind to build a product that might comfort individuals with Alzheimer’s in moments of high anxiety or stress. This idea was hatched Fall of 2015, and was inspired from noticing a drastic change in behavior of an individual with Alzheimer’s during a caretaker interview as the family pet walked in the room. Ever since this observation, we have been experimenting with stuffed animals filled with custom electronics that allow for interactions between the product and end-user. From tactile input, this device will softly vibrate and give off heat, just like an actual cat (see project gallery to the right)!
